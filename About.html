Our Story

STACKLY was founded in 2018 with a singular rebellion in mind: standard recruiting was too slow, too inaccurate, and entirely detached from the actual culture of modern engineering teams.


By leveraging proprietary matching algorithms combined with deep human behavioral insights, we've successfully scaled over 500+ startups and enterprise divisions. Today, we stand as the definitive bridge between world-class talent and structural innovation.
